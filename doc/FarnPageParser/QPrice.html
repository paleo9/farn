    parse(p) click to toggle source

    Extracts @quantity and @pounds and @pence. It looks for quantities like 25+ and prices like that begin with a Great British Pound Sign followed by a decimal number, denoting pounds and pence. Pence can be more than two decimal places.

    # File farn_page_parser.rb, line 275
def parse(p)
  pattern = /(?'qty'\d{1,})\+.*?£(?'pounds'\d{1,})\.(?'pence'\d{1,})/
  m = pattern.match p
  @quantity = m[:qty].to_i
  @pounds = m[:pounds].to_i
  @pence = m[:pence].to_i
end
